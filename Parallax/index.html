Parallax

Project created for learning and using the parallax effect.

Famous uses

Games and design

Here are two of the most famous uses of parallax, one of them is 2D games that marked generations like 'Super Mario' and 'Sonic' using the effect for better atmosphere and immersion. The other is the designs of major brands like Google and Apple that consequently opted for its use because of the smoothness and "lightness" it offers.

advantages

Beautiful and interactive

The advantages are significant in small actions, provoking small interactions, greater depth, and an image of greater professionalism.

care

Excessive and abusive use

Although aesthetically pleasing, excessive and overuse can cause problems such as: FPS drops, response issues, loading times, readability problems, and contrast issues.
